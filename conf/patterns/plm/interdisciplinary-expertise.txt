Interdisciplinary Expertise|2

Individuals are encouraged to develop inter-disciplinary skills. Encourage generalists who can easily specialize.

Specialists are often required for product implementations given the technological complexity of Telecommunications environments. The Telecom industry, however, is also characterized by rapid change, and there is a tendency for specialists to desire retaining their specializations despite these changes.

Therefore focus individuals within the enterprise to build expertise in multiple disciplines. Encourage data specialists to become process specialists, and process specialists to become business specialists. Encourage everyone to perform a multitude of varied tasks.

Develop a Few Skilled Gardeners who can tackle almost any aspect of the product, starting from Change it with Confidence.
